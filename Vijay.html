vijay

Vijay is an Indian actor and playback singer who works in Tamil cinema. He has won numerous awards, including four Tamil Nadu State Film Awards, three Filmfare Awards South, and a National Film Award. He is one of the highest-paid actors in Tamil cinema and has a large fan following.

Vijay made his acting debut as a child artist in the film "Vetri" (1984) and later appeared in several films as a child actor. He made his debut as a lead actor in the film "Naalaiya Theerpu" (1992) and has since acted in over 60 films. Some of his notable films include "Ghilli" (2004), "Thuppakki" (2012), "Mersal" (2017), and "Master" (2021).
